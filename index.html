Our Tradition

Take literatures or consider the stories which we used to listen from our grandparents. We can see that nature and its creation were an integral part of life. Human life has always found fascination with environment. When there was no one nature has already provided source of motivation to live a life of harmony and hustles.

“Spider failed severally before forming a net” a story which has supplied source of motivation from our childhood and it also shows that how our writers in past were into the nature completely. Various forms of nature are part of Indian culture call it Peepal Pooja or tulsi Pooja: which connects human life with trees and plants. In Chhath Pooja people worship near the river bank which connects human life with water bodies. No doubt our ancestors were more connected with nature than us,right!

Mission

Technology, Communication and Grounded Work

“Ya that tree has been cut down” We get surprised when we listen people saying this line with cold voice. It is not such due to their lack of interest but it gives alarming situation of people that how human life has disconnected with nature and this disconnection approach is increasing with time. Ofcourse in the modern era of technology human life has shrunk in limited area and that is the reason. Advising people on moral basis will not work any longer and older ways might not be effective as well. “Modern days and modern ways” a line itself indicates our motto.

Simple solution for solving this issue is “connect people with nature”. No one throws plastic in his house. No one allows outsider to cut the tree which has grown in front of their eyes. Even you felt this situation because in this case people is attached and aware of that place. Same way our mission is to connect people with nature. More we will connect people with nature lesser the issue will arise.

Vision

Generating community with same minded people

We focus with the grounded work and effective communication and we want to connect with every people. Who has genuinely an impulse within to work with nature and to build a community with them for working in various areas such as increasing green cover by planting trees , creating content for people about various issues and their applicable solution. Lets shake hand for to build conscious community and better tomorrow.

Plantation Drive

Well all it has been started without any thought of creating it a mission by simply planting these three plants at the river bank of Sabarmati near Dholeshvar mahadev mandir , Gandhinagar.

Nature has planned something different for us and after the cleaning drive at river on the next Sunday we have started planting trees and never looked back then.

And our friends from outside of Gandhinagar have joined us in our mission. Such as chhota udepur , vadodara and benglore.

Clean Up Drive

Rivers are the lifeline for all the creatures and it is needless to say that how our ancestors had developed the life near the rivers. Though in the modern era we have lost the touch and our sensitivity has decreased by the time as well. So we have initiated group activity first to give a little effort to clean up Sabarmati river.

After that, various activities such as clean up drive at forest near the river bank have been operated as well. While that process we actually got to know that real enemy of mother nature is plastic. Have a look towards our work. Yes we know that such work won’t solve the issue at large scale but every reservoir takes shape by individual drops.

In this work we are very grateful to the system. As of Gandhinagar municipality has extended its support by providing resources and solution too.

When intention of work is pure then flowers of recognition are likely to be bloomed. In newspaper such as Divyabhaskar, Gujarat samachar and Gandhinagar municipality have applaud our work and we are thankful to all of them.

Birds-Wirds

If you are tree lover or let say river lover then you must have developed deep relation with birds right!!! So as us.

Again with no expertise but with resources available around us we have created nests from discarded wires and pills of coconuts and guess what we have got guests at our doorsteps and then our inspiring guy Hardik suggested a way to create nests out of Garba . Which is a traditional part of our Indian culture and as usual plan has been executed. They were deposited in the wild and again nature has blessed our work by giving us glimpse of the birds habitat in our deposited nest.

Till now more than 55 nests have been deposited and 70+ water pots have been distributed. Which are helping our winged friends to thrive and propagate their numbers in this changing world.
